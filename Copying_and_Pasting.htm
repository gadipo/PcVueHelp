Copying and Pasting

To place a copy on the Clipboard

  1. Choose Select/Resize from the Draw toolbar.
  2. Select one or more drawing element.
  3. Click the Copy button on the Standard toolbar or press Ctrl-C on the keyboard.

To cut and place a copy on the Clipboard

  1. Choose Select/Resize from the Draw toolbar.
  2. Select one or more drawing element.
  3. Click the Cut button on the Standard toolbar or press Ctrl-C on the keyboard.

To duplicate

  1. Choose Select/Resize from the Draw toolbar.
  2. Press and hold the Control key on the keyboard.
  3. Click on the drawing element and holding down the mouse button drag the cursor.
  4. Release the mouse button. A copy of the drawing element will be inserted at the cursor location.

To paste the contents of the Clipboard in a window

  1. Click in the window to select it.
  2. Click the Paste button on the Standard toolbar or press Ctrl-V on the keyboard.

The contents of the Windows Clipboard will be pasted in the selected window. The location will be the same as that of the original drawing element.

To paste the contents of the Clipboard at a specific location

  1. Click in the window to select it.
  2. Display the Window Properties dialog.
  3. Select the Paste here command.

The contents of the Clipboard will be pasted at the current cursor position.

If the Windows Clipboard contains anything other than the HMI's native geometric drawing elements, the contents will be inserted as an OLE object.
