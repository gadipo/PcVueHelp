Blinking Rates

In most of the drawing elements and many of the animations, you can select a blinking color for one or more of the properties. A blinking color alternates between two selected colors at a pre-defined rate. You can change the rate from a dialog by selecting the menu Preferences.Blinking.

Show picture

There are 16 blink rates each of which has two configurable periods, one for each of the colors. To change either period, click and drag the appropriate slider control. If you are using blinking colors to attract the operator's attention it is recommended that you use a ratio of approximately 2 to 1 for the periods.

Each period may be configured within the range of 150ms to 2000 ms.
