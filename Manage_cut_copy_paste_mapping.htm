Using Cut, Copy and Paste with Mapping

If you cut and paste, or copy and paste, any variable where the source is mapped to one of PcVue data acquisition objects then the mapping is duplicated in the new variable. The mapping of more than one variable to the some data acquisition objects is not recommended therefore, after using copy and paste, the mapping of either the original variable, or the duplicate, must be edited.
