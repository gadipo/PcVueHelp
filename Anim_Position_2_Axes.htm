Two Axes Positioning

Two axes positioning is used to move a drawing according to the value of two registers. Two Axes positioning may be applied to all drawing elements. Show picture

The drawing movement is within an imaginary rectangle. One corner of the rectangle is defined by the position of the support drawing element. The opposite corner is defined by the position of the support drawing element plus the X and Y axis movement properties.

The X and Y axis movement properties may be positive or negative. Positive X axis values cause movement from the left to the right of the screen. Positive Y axis values cause movement from the top to the bottom of the screen.

The start and end point values can be any value within the range of the selected registers. The start value can be less than or greater than the end value.
