ScrollTo Method

 Applies to 

To scroll the window's contents to a position.

Syntax

Sub object. ScrollTo(Horizontal As Long, Vertical As Long[, WindowAs Integer])

Part

Description

Horizontal

The desired position on the horizontal scrollbar.

Vertical

The desired position on the vertical scroll bar.

Window

An Integer representing the window in which the operation is to take place. (Only used with the Mimic object).

The horizontal and vertical co-ordinates define the reference point of the mimic that must be placed, if possible, at the centre of the window after the scroll operation.
