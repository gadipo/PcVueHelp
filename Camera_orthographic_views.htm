3D Camera Orthographic views

In top, bottom, front, rear, left or right view camera mode, a window displays an orthographic projection of a scene, seen from the top, bottom, front, rear, left or right respectively. The following mouse actions are available.

Mouse action

Result

Hold down the right hand button and move sideways

Moves the camera horizontally. This changes the camera target.

Hold down the right hand button and move forward or backward

Moves the camera vertically. This changes the camera target.

Hold down the center mouse button and move sideways and/or forward & backward

Moves the camera so as to reduce or increase the distance between the camera and the target. The camera moves nearer to or farther from the target as you move the mouse to the left (or away) or to the right (or toward you) respectively.

Roll the central mouse wheel

As above.
