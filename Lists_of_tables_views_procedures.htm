Lists of Tables, Views and Stored Procedures

You can list the database tables, views and stored procedures of a database from the tree structure in the Database Manager.

If a '+' icon is not shown beside a name in the tree structure, click on the name. If there are any items to be listed within it, the '+' icon will appear after a short pause.

Displaying a table

  1. Click on the '+' icon of the required database's name to open its sub-trees. Show picture

  2. Click on the '+' icon of Tables to list its tables.
  3. Select a particular table to display its properties in the right-hand pane.
  4. Click on the '+' icon of a particular table to list its columns as a sub-tree.
  5. Select a column's name in the sub-tree to display its properties or contents in the right-hand pane.

Displaying a view

  1. Click on the '+' icon of the required database's name to open its sub-trees. Show picture

  2. Click on the '+' icon of Views to list its views.
  3. Select a particular view to display its properties in the right-hand pane.
  4. Click on the '+' icon of a particular view to list its columns as a sub-tree.
  5. Select a column's name in the sub-tree to display its properties or contents in the right-hand pane.

Displaying a stored procedure

  1. Click on the '+' icon of the required database's name to open its sub-tree. Show picture

  2. Click on the '+' icon of Views to list its stored procedures as a sub-tree.
  3. Select a particular stored procedure's name in the sub-tree to display its properties in the right-hand pane.
  4. If a stored procedure has a '+' icon, you can click on it to list its components.
  5. Select a component's name in the sub-tree to display its properties or contents in the right-hand pane.

For details of how to display and modify the contents a table, see the book Maintaining Database Contents.
