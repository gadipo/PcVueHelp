Moving the Camera

The camera is what gives you a particular view of the 3D mimic.

To shift the view (move the camera) right-click anywhere in the 3D window, keep the right mouse button pressed and drag the mouse in any direction. Your view rotates around its center. Imagine a camera positioned at your eye, the camera is actually in orbit around the center of the scene.
