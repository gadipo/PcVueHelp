Trend Viewer Example

This example exercises a method and several properties of the Trend Viewer:

Private Sub Run_VBA_for_Trend_Click()

 

'METHOD:

 

'show the About box:

Trend1.AboutBox

 

'PROPERTIES:

 

'change background colour to yellow then blue

Trend1.BackColor = vbYellow

MsgBox ("Background colour was: " & Trend1.BackColor)

Trend1.BackColor = vbBlue

 

'toggle the time information below the scale bar:

Trend1.DisplayTimeInfo = Not Trend1.DisplayTimeInfo

 

'toggle the play mode (play/pause):

Trend1.PlayMode = Not Trend1.PlayMode

 

'change the refresh period:

MsgBox ("Refresh Period was: " & Trend1.RefreshPeriod)

Trend1.RefreshPeriod = Trend1.RefreshPeriod * 2

MsgBox ("Refresh Period is now: " & Trend1.RefreshPeriod)

 

'toggle the scale bar (visible/hidden):

Trend1.scalebar = Not Trend1.scalebar

 

'toggle the scale line (visible/hidden):

Trend1.ScaleLine = Not Trend1.ScaleLine

 

'toggle the toolbar (visible/hidden):

Trend1.toolbar = Not Trend1.toolbar

 

MsgBox ("To show or hide the bars, run the script again.")

 

End Sub
