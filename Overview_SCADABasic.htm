Overview of SCADA Basic

See also

User programs can be written in a scripting language known as SCADA Basic. This is a block structured, interpreted language with a syntax closely related to industry standard Basic.

A project may be configured to run programs on various occasions:

  • At start-up of PcVue.
  • On change of a variable in the database.
  • Cyclically.
  • From the keyboard.
  • From control zones on the screen.
  • When called from other programs.
