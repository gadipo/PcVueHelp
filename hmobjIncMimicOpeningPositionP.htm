IncMimicOpeningPosition Property

 Applies to 

Description

Returns or sets the attribute that indicates the position of a newly opened included mimic according to the animation that has caused its opening. Read/Write.

Syntax

object.IncMimicOpeningPosition [= fvOpenPosition]

IncMimicOpeningPosition can take one of the following fvOpenPosition values:

fvOpenPositionNone
fvOpenPositionTopLeftCorner
fvOpenPositionBottomLeftCorner
fvOpenPositionTopRightCorner
fvOpenPositionBottomRightCorner
fvOpenPositionOnObject
fvOpenPositionBottomObject
fvOpenPositionLeftObject
fvOpenPositionTopObject
fvOpenPositionRightObject
fvOpenPositionBottomRightObject
