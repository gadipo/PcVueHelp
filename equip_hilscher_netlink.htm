Hilscher netLINK Ip

This driver supports interfacing with Hilscher netLINK Ip adapters. Thanks to netLINK, PcVue can communicate via Ethernet with Siemens S7-300 or S7-400 PLCs. The cable can be connected to either the PLC's Profibus or MPI port. In all exchanges, PcVue is the client and the PLC is the server.

netLINK is a protocol converter that takes the form of a cable with an RJ45 Ethernet connector one end and a Profibus connector the other. The electronics required for the protocol conversion is located in the housing of the Profibus connector.

The netLINK hardware is available under different forms:

  • NL-MPI - For use when connecting to a single PLC.
  • NL50-MPI - Like the NL-MPI but with an RJ45 connector.
  • NT40-MPI - For use when connecting to multiple PLC's up to a maximum of 32.

The netLINK cable can also be used for programming the S7 PLC using a driver from the company IBH Softec.

For installation and configuration of netLINK, refer to the documentation supplied by Hilscher.

The PLC variables in the SIMATIC S7 range (S7-400, S7-300) use a byte addressing mode.

S7 messaging with Hilscher netLINK can be used to read a number of words, double or floating words with byte, word, or double word alignments. These alignment modes enable you to obtain words or double words with even or odd addresses.
