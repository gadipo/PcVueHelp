MQTT Add-on traces

Information regarding the MQTT Add-on are logged in the traces and displayed in the Event Viewer with lines starting with '[MQTT]'.

Additional traces can be triggered using the SCADA Basic instruction TRACE as follows.

TRACE(Mode, ManagerID, HexFlag);
  • Mode - Turn the additional trace on or off
    • 1 - On
    • 0 - Off
  • ManagerID - The identifier of the MQTT Add-on task. Set to 11 if MQTT is the only Add-on in use.
  • HexFlag - Hexadecimal combination flag
    • 001 - To trace received payloads only
    • 002 - To trace sent payloads only

Example of use of SCADA Basic TRACE instruction

TRACE(1,11,”001”);

Turn on additional trace for received payloads
TRACE(1,11,”002”);

Turn on additional trace for sent payloads
TRACE(1,11,”003”); Turn on additional trace for received and sent payloads
TRACE(0,11,”002”); Turn off additional trace for sent payloads

TRACE(0,11,”003”);

Turn off additional trace for received and sent payloads
