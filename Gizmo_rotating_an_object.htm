Rotating a Model

You can use the circular shape around each axis to rotate a model.

  1. Select a model.
  2. Click and hold on the circle.
  3. Drag the mouse to rotate the model around the corresponding local axis:

The motion is as follows:

  • Forward or rightward to rotate one way.
  • Backward and leftward to counter-rotate.
